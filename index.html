The story.

Boondocking is for the adventurers. The inquisitive and the fearless. You seek out the unknown and revel in those chance meetings and once-in-a-lifetime opportunities. Traditional campsites bore you. To hell with designated parking spots. To hell with fenced off firepits. You pave your own path and that doesn't come with a pricetag.
